Cybersecurity Law Interpretation

Published: 2024-01-10 Author: Compliance Expert Team Reading Time: ~15 minutes

Summary: This article provides a detailed interpretation of the core requirements of China's Cybersecurity Law, including data localization, cybersecurity review, and other key provisions, helping foreign enterprises understand the cybersecurity regulations they need to comply with when conducting business in China.

I. Legal Background

The "Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China" was passed on November 7, 2016, and officially implemented on June 1, 2017. This is China's first comprehensive foundational law regulating cyberspace security management, providing legal basis for cyberspace governance.

II. Core Requirements

2.1 Data Localization

According to Article 37 of the Cybersecurity Law, operators of critical information infrastructure must store personal information and important data collected and generated during their operations in China within the territory. If business needs require providing data overseas, security assessment must be conducted according to measures formulated by the national cyberspace administration in conjunction with relevant departments of the State Council.

Impact on Foreign Enterprises: If your business involves critical information infrastructure, you need to ensure data is stored within China, and security assessment is required before data export.

2.2 Cybersecurity Review

The cybersecurity review system requires security review of procurement activities of critical information infrastructure that may affect national security, ensuring supply chain security.

2.3 Personal Information Protection

Network operators collecting and using personal information should follow the principles of legality, legitimacy, and necessity, publicly disclose collection and use rules, clearly state the purpose, method, and scope of information collection and use, and obtain consent from the data subject.

三、合规建议

立即行动

  • • 评估业务是否涉及关键信息基础设施
  • • 建立数据本地化存储方案
  • • 完善个人信息保护制度

长期规划

  • • 建立网络安全管理制度
  • • 定期进行安全评估
  • • 加强员工网络安全培训

四、常见问题

Q: 我的公司是否属于关键信息基础设施？

A: 关键信息基础设施包括能源、交通、水利、金融、公共服务、电子政务等重要行业和领域。建议咨询专业法律顾问进行具体评估。

Q: 数据出境需要什么程序？

A: 需要向国家网信部门申请安全评估，评估通过后方可出境。具体流程和材料要求请参考相关实施细则。
